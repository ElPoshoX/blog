My name is Azgad García Zúñiga, but the *bandita* calls me **Az** for short and because it's hard to pronounce my name lol, and I have a nickname acquired since I was young which is “poshox” which I decided to keep just for nostalgia.

With **12+ years of experience**, I have studies in **computer science** (bachelors and masters) and at some point I plan to finally do my PhD in the same.

**I really enjoy solving any engineering or product problem I can**, as well as helping people solve theirs, always thinking that I wish I had had someone like that when I was aimless in my early years.

I've been **Head of SRE** and **Head of Engineering**, and have worked across industries like **fintech** (at scale — ~1,500 engineers, ~3,000 microservices), **healthcare** and **retail**. My areas of expertise are **Cloud**, **DevOps**, **Platform Engineering**, **Chaos Engineering** and **SRE**, though I've also worked as a Software Developer, DBA and SysAdmin. Day to day I work with **Kubernetes**, **Go**, **AWS**, **Terraform/Terragrunt**, **ArgoCD** and **GitOps** in general.

My career thesis: **every company is a tech company, some just haven't accepted it yet**.

I contribute to **open source** — Backstage community plugins and internal tools that end up becoming their own projects. Lately I've been exploring **local LLM inference** and **AI infrastructure**.

I currently work as **Principal Engineer** and provide external consulting on any of the other topics. :rocket:

When I'm not in the terminal, I'm probably tinkering with my **homelab** — Proxmox, Talos Linux, UniFi networking, VLANs, BGP... it's probably more complex than some production environments I've seen.

Based in **Guadalajara, México**. Find me as **@ElPoshoX** everywhere.

This blog covers platform engineering, DevOps, SRE, IaC, and whatever technical rabbit hole I fall into.
